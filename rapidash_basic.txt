EMBER
    Basic Move    Fire
    Standard Action    Ranged 10
    Target: One Pokemon
    Attack: Dexterity vs. Reflex
    Hit: 1d8 + Dexterity modifier Fire damage

FLAME WHEEL
    Basic Move    Fire
    Standard Action    Melee
    Target: One Pokemon
    Attack: Strength vs. Reflex
    Hit: 1d6 + Strength modifier Fire damage, and
      the target is pushed a number of squares up
      to your Strength modifier

FIRE SPIN
    Basic Move    Fire
    Standard Action    Burst 1 within 7
    Target: All Pokemon in burst
    Attack: Intelligence vs. Reflex
    Hit: 1d4 Fire damage
        Sustain Standard: The burst becomes a zone
          of fire that deals 1d6 + Intelligence
          modifier Fire damage to any Pokemon that
          enters the zone or starts its turn
          within the zone

LOW KICK
    Basic Move    Fighting
    Standard Action    Melee
    Target: One Pokemon
    Attack: Dexterity vs. Reflex
    Hit: 1d6 + Dexterity modifier Fighting damage
      and the target grants advantage to the next
      attack made against it until the start of
      your next turn
