POUND
    Basic Move    Normal
    Standard Action    Melee
    Target: One Pokemon
    Attack: Dexterity vs. AC
    Hit: 1d6 + Dexterity modifier Normal damage
      and you may shift one square as a free
      action either before or after the attack

SLUDGE
    Basic Move    Poison
    Standard Action    Ranged 10
    Target: One Pokemon
    Attack: Dexterity vs. Reflex
    Hit: 1d8 + Dexterity modifier Poison damage

PAYBACK
    Basic Move    Dark
    Standard Action    Melee
    Target: One Pokemon
    Attack: Strength vs. AC
    Hit: 1d6 + Strength modifier DARK damage
    Extra: If the target dealt damage to you since
      your last turn, deal an extra 1d4 Dark
      damage on this attack

ROCK BLAST
    Basic Move    Rock
    Standard Action    Burst 1 within 7 squares
    Target: All Pokemon in burst
    Attack: Strength vs. AC
    Hit: 1d4 + Strength modifier Rock damage

ROLLOUT
    Basic Move    Rock
    Standard Action    Melee
    Target: One Pokemon
    Attack: Constitution vs. AC
    Hit: 1d4 + Constitution modifier Rock damage
    Extra: You may move up to half your movement
      speed before the attack as a free action
