TACKLE
    Basic Move    Normal
    Standard Action    Melee
    Target: One Pokemon
    Attack: Constitution vs. AC
    Hit: 1d6 + Constitution modifier Normal
      damage, and you push the target a number of
      squares up to your Constitution modifier

VINE WHIP
    Basic Move    Grass
    Standard Action    Ranged 7
    Target: One Pokemon
    Attack: Strength vs. AC
    Hit: 1d8 + Strength modifier Grass damage

GRASS KNOT
    Basic Move    Grass
    Standard Action    Melee
    Target: One Pokemon
    Attack: Constitution vs. Reflex
    Hit: 1d4 + Constitution modifier Grass damage
      and the target is dazed until the start of
      your next turn

SLUDGE
    Basic Move    Poison
    Standard Action    Ranged 10
    Target: One Pokemon
    Attack: Dexterity vs. Reflex
    Hit: 1d8 + Dexterity modifier Poison damage
