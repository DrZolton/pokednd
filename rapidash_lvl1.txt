GROWL
    Level 1    Normal
    Minor Action    Ranged 5
    Target: One Pokemon
    Attack: Charisma vs. Will
    Effect: The target takes a -2 penalty to
      damage rolls based in Constitution
      Strength, and Dexterity (minimum 1) until
      the end of the encounter

    Target: One Pokemon
