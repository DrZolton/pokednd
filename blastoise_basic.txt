TACKLE
    Basic Move    Normal
    Standard Action    Melee
    Target: One Pokemon
    Attack: Constitution vs. AC
    Hit: 1d6 + Constitution modifier Normal
      damage, and you push the target a number of
      squares up to your Constitution modifier

WATER GUN
    Basic Move    Water
    Standard Action    Ranged 10
    Target: One Pokemon
    Attack: Dexterity vs. AC
    Hit: 1d8 + Dexterity modifier Water damage

BUBBLE
    Basic Move    Water
    Standard Action    Blast 2
    Target: All Pokemon in blast
    Attack: Dexterity vs. Reflex
    Hit: 1d4 + Dexterity modifier Water damage

BITE
    Basic Move    Dark
    Standard Action    Melee
    Target: One Pokemon
    Attack: Strength vs. AC
    Hit: 1d8 + Strength modifier DARK damage

BULLDOZE
    Basic Move    Ground
    Standard Action    Blast 2
    Target: All Pokemon in blast
    Attack: Strength vs. Fortitude
    Hit: 1d4 Ground damage, and the target is
      pushed one square

AQUA JET
    Basic Move    Water
    Standard Action    Melee
    Target: One Pokemon
    Attack: Strength vs. AC
    Hit: 1d6 + Strength modifier Water damage
    Extra: You may move a number of squares up to
      your Strength modifier before the attack as
      a free action
