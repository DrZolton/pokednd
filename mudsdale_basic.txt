MUD-SLAP
    Basic Move    Ground
    Standard Action    Melee
    Target: One Pokemon
    Attack: Strength vs. Reflex
    Hit: 1d6 + Strength modifier Ground damage
      and the target has disadvantage to the next
      attack it makes before the start of your
      next turn

BULLDOZE
    Basic Move    Ground
    Standard Action    Blast 2
    Target: All Pokemon in blast
    Attack: Strength vs. Fortitude
    Hit: 1d4 Ground damage, and the target is
      pushed one square

PAYBACK
    Basic Move    Dark
    Standard Action    Melee
    Target: One Pokemon
    Attack: Strength vs. AC
    Hit: 1d6 + Strength modifier DARK damage
    Extra: If the target dealt damage to you since
      your last turn, deal an extra 1d4 Dark
      damage on this attack
