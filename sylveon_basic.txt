FAIRY WIND
    Basic Move    Fairy
    Standard Action    Close blast 2
    Target: All Pokemon in blast
    Attack: Wisdom vs. Fortitude
    Hit: 1d4 + Wisdom modifier Fairy damage

DISARMING VOICE
    Basic Move    Fairy
    Standard Action    Close burst 1
    Target: All Pokemon in burst
    Attack: Charisma vs. Will
    Hit: Charisma modifier Fairy damage, and the
      target grants advantage to the next attack
      made against it until the start of your next
      turn

TACKLE
    Basic Move    Normal
    Standard Action    Melee
    Target: One Pokemon
    Attack: Constitution vs. AC
    Hit: 1d6 + Constitution modifier Normal
      damage, and you push the target a number of
      squares up to your Constitution modifier

DRAINING KISS
    Basic Move    Fairy
    Standard Action    Melee
    Target: One Pokemon
    Attack: Charisma vs. Will
    Hit: 1d4 + Charisma modifier Fairy damage, and
      you heal 1d4 hitpoints
